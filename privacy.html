Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Here's how we handle your data on Tulvaa.

1. Data Collection and Processing

Tulvaa is designed as a privacy-first application. All data processing (such as image compression, PDF creation, and text encryption) occurs directly on your device using your browser. We do not upload your files or text to our servers.

2. Cookies and Local Storage

We use local storage for saving your theme preferences and favorites, but do not use tracking cookies or analytics.

  • Functional Cookies: Only for theme, favorites, and basic preferences.
  • Analytics: We do not currently use third-party analytics services.

3. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to other websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices or content.

4. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to individuals under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions, contact us at contact@Tulvaa.in.
